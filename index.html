From the sky

Chicago has two major airports: O’Hare (ORD) and Midway (MDW). Most international flights come into O’Hare. Midway is a Southwest Airlines hub, which typically has great prices for flights across the U.S. Make sure you check Southwest's website, as they are not listed in most flight search engines.

Both airports are around 15 miles from Oak Brook. You can rent a car, take a taxi, or call a car. Lyft and Uber will both cost about $40.

From Chicago

Oak Brook is 20 miles west of Downtown Chicago along the Eisenhower Expressway. There are two commuter rail routes that get you from downtown Chicago to the Oak Brook area in about half an hour:

  • Union Pacific-West line from Chicago Ogilvie Transport Center to Elmhurst
  • BNSF line from Chicago Union Station to Hinsdale
From the train stations, it's about a 10 minute taxi or Lyft/Uber to Oak Brook.
